New York City: Place Matters

New York City is famous for skyscrapers, Times Square, Central Park, the Statue of Liberty. But New York is actually an aggregation of countless places, both famous and obscure, that together weave a colorful tapestry of activity, culture, history, and memory.

In 1998 City Lore and the Municipal Art Society launched Place Matters to foster greater appreciation of New York’s hugely diverse local establishments and cherished sites. Place Matters celebrates these locations and advocates for their preservation. Use this map to explore these places, and to learn why they matter.
